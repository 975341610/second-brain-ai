OpenAI Launches $100 Pro Subscription Tier Between Plus and the $200 Plan
=========================================================================

[OpenAI](https://www.ghacks.net/category/ai/openai-ai/) has launched a new **$100 per month** Pro subscription for [ChatGPT](https://www.ghacks.net/2026/03/24/openai-introduces-chatgpt-library-to-store-uploaded-files-in-one-place/), creating a middle option between the existing $20 Plus and $200 plans. This new tier caters to users who depend on advanced AI tools for complex, recurring tasks and directly competes with Anthropic's $100 Max plan, which has gained popularity among coding-focused users.

Updated ChatGPT Subscription Tiers and What They Include
--------------------------------------------------------

OpenAI offers three subscription tiers. The tiers:

* **Plus plan costs $20 per month** and is suitable for lighter use, providing access to advanced features like [Codex](https://www.ghacks.net/2026/04/03/openai-adds-pay-as-you-go-codex-seats-for-chatgpt-business-and-enterprise-teams/) and Deep Research for select projects during the week
* **Pro plan is priced at $100 per month** and is designed for sustained use of advanced tools and models, with usage limits five times higher than Plus and, for a limited time, Codex usage ten times higher than Plus.
* **The highest tier, priced at $200 per month**, is intended for continuous, demanding workflows across multiple projects, offering usage limits twenty times higher than Plus

All three plans include access to Pro models, Codex, Deep Research, image creation, Memory, and file uploads.

OpenAI states that the $100 Pro plan provides unlimited access to GPT-5 and legacy models, although standard Terms of Use still apply, including restrictions on account sharing. The tenfold increase in Codex usage compared to Plus is described as a limited-time offer with no end date specified.

How the New $100 Pro Tier Fits Into the AI Pricing Landscape
------------------------------------------------------------

OpenAI previously did not offer a tier between $20 and $200, leaving a gap of $180. Anthropic's offerings include a $20 plan and a $100 Max plan, along with a $200 tier. OpenAI has not commented on whether the new Pro tier was created in response to Anthropic's pricing, but the $100 price point and focus on Codex usage closely resemble Anthropic's competing plan.
